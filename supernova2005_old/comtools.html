Supernova is more than just a conference. The physical gathering in Santa Clara is only one element of an interactive, networked community. Using cutting-edge online community tools, we facilitate a parallel virtual event that extends Supernova in time, space, and participation.

Most of the tools below are accessible to non-attendees. Though there is no substitute for the on-site experience, we value participation from those who could not attend this year.

Conference weblog
The Supernova blog will provide conference coverage as well as commentary by a distinguished group of expert bloggers. Before the event, it will serve as a discusion forum and information resource.
