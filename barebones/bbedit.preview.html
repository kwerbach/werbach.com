Welcome to the gateway page for the Bare Bones Guide to HTML. The Guide lists every tag in the official HTML 3.2 specification, plus the Netscape extensions, in a concise, organized format. The latest version of the Bare Bones Guide to HTML is always available at http://werbach.com/barebones/. Comments and suggestions are welcome. While you're here, take time out to visit the -k- page, featuring the Whirrled Project, the Home Page Hall of Fame, and lots of other original content!

Additional information for Web page designers is available through my WWW Help Page. Please check that page before you email any questions to me. Due to the large volume of email I receive, I can't guarantee a speedy response.

You are welcome to redistribute the Bare Bones Guide to HTML for any noncommercial purpose, or to put a link to the Guide on your own page, but please do not modify it in any way or place the files on your own server without permission. If you wish to include the Guide in a commercial project such as a book, please contact me; I usually grant permission upon request.

I am not affiliated with Bare Bones Software, makers of the excellent BBEdit text editor; the similarity of names is coincidental.

English-Language Files

Introduction Page
Includes a version history and some useful explanatory material about how the Guide is formatted, what makes it unique, and which tags are included.

Plain Text (v. 3.0)
The one that started it all. This version is designed for printing out or keeping open in a text editor while you design Web pages. It has no hypertext links or background information.

Formatted (v. 3.0)
Formatted using HTML tables, and also includes links to introductory material and annotations on some tags. The best-looking version for on-screen viewing or printing, assuming that your browser supports tables.
