Connecting Worlds Through Connected Platforms

Technological and business trends are converging to change the way we work, live, and play. Did you know that:

Supernova 2005 addresses the causes and implications of these startling developments. Our sessions and workshops will cover important topics poised to jump from the leading edge to the mainstream, including:

Wharton West Workshops -- Monday June 20

At the Wharton West Workshops and Technology Showcase, leading innovators and subject experts on distributed technologies will provide hands-on exposure to the latest connected business tools and trends. This day-long event will offer interactive seminars targeted to business professionals and technology strategists.

Workshops include:

  • Connected Work (with former IBM Internet guru John Patrick and executives from Socialtext, Traction Software, and NextPage)
  • A Whole New Internet? (featuring Adaptive Path CEO Janice Fraser, and an open roundtable discussion among leading next-generation application creators on the emerging web platform)
  • Business Blogging (with noted blogger Suw Charman, Microsoft blogging evangelist Robert Scoble, Forrester Research analyst Charlene Li, and Michael Sippey of blog software provider Six Apart)
  • The Long Tail (with Wired Editor in Chief Chris Anderson and leading e-commerce innovators)
  • TrueVoice: The Business of Social Media (with Stowe Boyd of Corante and Peter Quintas of SilkRoad Technology)
  • Decentralized Commerce (presented by CommerceNet, a track of sessions on innovative new technologies allowing myriad small players across entire industries to connect directly)
  • Conversational Marketing (featuring former Excite@Home chief marketing officer Susan Bratton, Jonathan Carson of BuzzMetrics, and Pete Blackshaw of Intelliseek)
  • Virtual Worlds, Real Money (with commentator Betsy Book, Sennari CEO Bill Barhydt, Georgia Tech researcher Ian Bogost, and Second Life developer Cory Ondrejka)

For those unable to attend the entire conference, a single-day rate for the workshops is available on our registration page. All conference attendees are invited to the kick-off Reception and Technology Showcase, the evening of June 20.

And that doesn't even include our stand-alone Perspectives and Spotlight talks, featuring some of the brightest minds in the industry.
