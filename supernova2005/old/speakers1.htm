Becoming a speaker

Most Supernova speaking engagements are extended by invitation only, from the conference host directly to participants. However, we do consider submissions from select companies or industry gurus who offer interesting innovations or important insights in the area of connected technologies.

If you would like us to evaluate high-level speakers and topics for Supernova, please submit your information to: speaking@supernovagroup.net. There is no formal submission deadline. However, the earlier we receive submissions, the more flexibility we have to consider inclusion.

Due to Supernova's popularity, we receive many more speaking requests than we can accommodate. For companies seeking exposure to Supernova's extended audience, we encourage you to contact us at sponsor@supernovagroup.net for information about sponsorship opportunities.
