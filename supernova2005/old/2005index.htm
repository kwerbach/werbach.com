Welcome to Supernova 2005!
June 20-22, San Francisco, CA

At Supernova, over 250 prominent technologists, investors, business leaders and media come together to network, share ideas, and explore the business impacts of key innovations.

Supernova focuses on the decentralization of computing, communications, digital media, and business.

Increasingly powerful devices, coupled with increasingly pervasive networks, are changing the way we work, live, and play. Supernova distills what those trends mean for you and your organization. Produced in partnership with The Wharton School, it uniquely links technology vision with practical business focus.

Subscribe to our Supernova Report email list for periodic updates and analysis.


2005 Theme: Connecting Worlds
Through Connected Platforms

In a decentralized world, the network itself is the platform for commerce, social interaction, work and entertainment. We'll explore the next generation of connected technologies in social and business contexts. Topics include:

distributed e-commerce • mobile applications • the new power of the "long tail" in commerce and media • massively multiplayer virtual worlds • business blogging • the video Internet • attention in a connected world • rich media collaboration • voice over IP applications • and more

We'll examine how corporations and individuals leverage social software, mobile devices, and online gaming environments, as well as ways that communities can maximize the latent value of their network connections.

New this year... a lot!

At Supernova, we don't just talk about the potential of a connected world, we experience it. Some of the innovative new experiences we'll feature this year include:

  • The Wharton West Workshops and Technology Showcase -- a day of emerging technology workshops targeted to technologists and business professionals, at the state-of-the-art Wharton West facility.
  • A "virtual party" happening simultaneously in San Francisco and in a massively multi-player online game, Second Life.
  • The first-ever conference video blogcast, presented by AT&T. The blogcast will be distributed through CNET News.com and Knowledge@Wharton.
  • The uberblog -- a unique custom-integrated suite of interactive community tools, including weblog syndication feeds, video streams, photos, and audio podcasts.
  • Spotlight talks, featuring innovators from business, academia, the military, the research community, and the media.
  • And more! Because we highlight the latest technologies and trends, we're adding new content and programs until just before the event.
