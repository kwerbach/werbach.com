Advocates for "open spectrum" approaches (unlicensed or spectrum-sharing models) as a solution to burgeoning wireless demand, positing that removing antiquated licensing constraints could unleash innovation and investment in broadband wireless technologies (the "great hope" for next-gen connectivity).
