## Conference Overview

Held in Philadelphia, PA in 2015, this conference examined "gameful" (gamification) strategies across education, business, and social impact sectors.

## Key Focus

Researchers and practitioners shared insights on how game design elements can boost user motivation and engagement, while discussing the ethical and practical considerations involved in applying these techniques at scale, ensuring gamification is used responsibly and effectively.
