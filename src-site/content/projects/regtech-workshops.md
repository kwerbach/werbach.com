## Overview

From 2017 to 2019, Professor Werbach hosted a series of high-level Reg@Tech workshops at the Wharton School, bringing together regulators, legal experts, and industry leaders from around the world to discuss the rapidly evolving landscape of cryptocurrencies and digital assets.

## Focus Areas

The workshop series addressed critical regulatory challenges including:

- **Token Sales and ICOs**: Examining the regulatory framework for initial coin offerings and token generation events
- **Decentralized Finance (DeFi)**: Exploring the implications of decentralized financial systems for existing regulatory structures
- **Global Regulatory Coordination**: Facilitating dialogue between regulators from different jurisdictions
- **Investor Protection**: Balancing innovation with consumer protection in digital asset markets

## Participants

The workshops featured:
- Senior regulators from the SEC, CFTC, and international counterparts
- Leading legal scholars specializing in financial regulation
- Industry practitioners and entrepreneurs
- Academic researchers in blockchain and financial technology

## Impact

These roundtables helped shape the conversation around cryptocurrency regulation during a critical period of market development and regulatory uncertainty, fostering dialogue between regulators, academics, and industry stakeholders.
