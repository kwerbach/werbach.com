## Colloquium Overview

Annual academic colloquium held at Wharton from 2004-2010, bringing together scholars and practitioners to discuss cutting-edge legal issues in media and communications.

## Key Themes

The colloquium addressed topics ranging from early internet content liability to mobile privacy, network neutrality, and the evolution of telecommunications regulation, fostering interdisciplinary dialogue at the nexus of law, technology, and business over its seven-year run.
