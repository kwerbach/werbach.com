## Workshop Overview

Held in Philadelphia, PA in 2025 as part of the Wharton Accountable AI Lab's activities, this workshop examined how principles and frameworks from the financial regulatory world can inform the governance of AI systems.

## Focus Areas

Drawing parallels between managing systemic risk in finance and ensuring safe AI deployment, the workshop explored risk management approaches, stress testing methodologies, and oversight mechanisms that could be adapted for AI governance.
