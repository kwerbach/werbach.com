## Conference Overview

Held in Brussels, Belgium in 2025, Reg@Tech Europe convened regulators, academics, and industry leaders to discuss the intersection of regulation and technology.

## Focus

The conference focused on European perspectives in fintech, blockchain, and AI regulation, fostering dialogue on innovative regulatory tools for the digital economy and exploring how regulators can keep pace with rapid technological change.
