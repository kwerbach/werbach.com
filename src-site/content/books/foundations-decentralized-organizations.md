## About the Book

*Foundations of Decentralized Organizations: Blockchain and the Future of Corporate Law* (edited with Eva Micheler and Bianca Kremer) provides a comprehensive analysis of how decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs) and other Web3 institutions are reshaping organizational structures, governance mechanisms, and legal frameworks.

Written by a global team of scholars from the United States, United Kingdom, continental Europe, Australia, and Asia, the book combines blockchain research with legal expertise.

## Key Themes

- Organizational design in decentralized systems
- Governance mechanisms for DAOs
- Legal status and regulatory challenges
- Token economics and incentive alignment
- Case studies of successful and failed DAOs
- The future of decentralized institutions
