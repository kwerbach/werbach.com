Reports on FCC initiatives in 2003 to open more spectrum for unlicensed use (e.g., in 5 GHz band) and support public WiFi, metaphorically saying the FCC "bakes up" fresh spectrum opportunities to enable more free or affordable wireless internet connections for the public.
