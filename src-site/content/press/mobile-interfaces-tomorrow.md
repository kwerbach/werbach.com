Looks at experimental mobile interfaces (circa 2000), from early smartphone concepts to novel input methods, predicting how tomorrow's mobile devices would move beyond tiny keypads and styluses to more natural interactions (via touch, voice, and context-awareness) – many of which materialized in later smartphones.
