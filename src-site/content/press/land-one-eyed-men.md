Uses the proverb "in the land of the blind, the one-eyed man is king" to comment on the telecom industry, suggesting that in an era of transformative change (like VoIP's emergence), those with even limited vision (willingness to embrace new tech) can lead, as many incumbents were "blind" to the changes.
