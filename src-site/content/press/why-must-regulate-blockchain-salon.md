Makes the case that government regulation of blockchain technology is necessary to prevent harms (like fraud, money laundering, and ecological damage from mining) and to integrate blockchain into the mainstream economy, pushing back against the idea that blockchain's decentralized nature makes it immune to or incompatible with regulation.
