A rallying cry for global unlicensed spectrum usage – "Spectrum of the world, unite!" – encouraging international moves to allocate more unlicensed spectrum and harmonize spectrum policies so that wireless innovations (like Wi-Fi) can thrive worldwide without fragmentation.
