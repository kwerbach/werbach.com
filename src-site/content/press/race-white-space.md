Discusses the emerging opportunity of using TV "white spaces" (unused broadcast spectrum) for unlicensed wireless broadband, describing it as a race among tech companies to exploit these frequencies and predicting it could spark a new wave of wireless innovation (an early take on what became "Super Wi-Fi").
