Explains how the Mueller investigation's indictments of Russian operatives for hacking (and their use of Bitcoin to finance operations) illustrate both the pseudonymous nature of Bitcoin transactions and the ways law enforcement can still track illicit crypto payments, undermining the myth of Bitcoin as completely untraceable.
