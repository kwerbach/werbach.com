Profiles the ecosystem of "e-commerce enablers" – companies providing the software and services behind online marketplaces – and explains what elements are necessary to "make a market" online, such as trust infrastructure, payment systems, logistics, and community, illustrating how these enablers underpinned the dot-com economy.
