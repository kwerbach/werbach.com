About the Show

Technology · Business · Policy

The Digital Show was a weekly podcast and SiriusXM radio program that explored the intersection of technology, business, and public policy. Hosted by three rotating hosts from the worlds of academia, government, and media, each episode featured conversations with leading thinkers, entrepreneurs, journalists, and policymakers.

Guests ranged from Gavin Wood (CTO of Ethereum) and Tom Kalil (White House OSTP) to leading journalists at Fortune, CNET, Forbes, and NPR. Topics spanned the sharing economy, spectrum policy, artificial intelligence, e-commerce, gamification, blockchain, and more.

The show aired 25 episodes between January 2015 and January 2016. The original website was at digitalshow111.com.
