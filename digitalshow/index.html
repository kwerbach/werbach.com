About the Show

The Digital Show was a weekly radio program on SiriusXM Channel 111 (Wharton Business Radio), airing Mondays 5–7pm ET. The show was also available as a podcast.

Each episode featured conversations with leading thinkers, entrepreneurs, journalists, and policymakers on technology, business, and public policy.

The show ran from January 2015 through January 2016.
