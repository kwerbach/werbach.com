Speaker Submission
Supernova speakers are generally selected by invitation, to produce the best mix of people and content. We do not have a formal call for papers or submission deadline. We welcome speaker proposals by email, although, due to Supernova's popularity, we are unable to accommodate the vast majority of requests.
