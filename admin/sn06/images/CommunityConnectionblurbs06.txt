<b>Supernova Weblog</b> -- a dedicated blog that covers Supernova Conference events, topics and activities.
<br /><br />
<b>Blogcast</b> -- a video-based conference blog authored by on-site reporter, David Weinberger; coverage by CNET News.com and Knowledge@Wharton. 
<br /><br />
<b>IRC Chat</b> -- a real-time interactive forum for the Supernova Community to discuss and follow session topics.  Freenode channel #supernova.
<br /><br />
<b>Eventspace Wiki</b> -- a central place for Conference participants to exchange information, coordinate activities and provide materials.
<br /><br />
<b>Jookster</b> -- a community-based search tool for sharing information and interacting on common interests.
<br /><br />
<b>SynchroEdit</b> -- a Web-based collaborative content editing tool (requires Firefox).
<br /><br />
<b>Ask.com QuickLinks</b> -- a way to easily view and access the most popular Supernova Web searches.
<br /><br />
<b>Email Directory</b> -- a list of Supernova-generated email addresses for all attendees.
