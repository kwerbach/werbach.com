The Supernova conference addresses the business and social implications of our increasingly connected world, cutting across Internet-based services, telecommunications, digital media, public policy, and business strategy. Organized by Kevin Werbach and produced in partnership with The Wharton School, it attracts top industry influencers, leading business executives, intellectual trend-setters, and pioneering entrepreneurs.
