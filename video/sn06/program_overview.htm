Supernova 2006 will offer three full days of conference programs and special events.

 


Wharton West Events: June 21st

At the Wharton West Workshops, leading innovators and subject experts on distributed technologies provide hands-on exposure to the latest connected business tools and trends. The workshops are held at Wharton's state-of-the-art downtown San Francisco facility.

This day-long event offers interactive seminars targeted to business professionals and technology strategists. Check out the 2005 workshop schedule for details about the previous conference; information on 2006 workshops will be available soon.

The day concludes with our opening reception and technology showcase, open to all Supernova conference participants.

 



Main Conference: June 22nd - 23rd

At Supernova 2006, we'll once again bring together technology's top thought leaders and innovators, strategic business professionals, leading investors, policy-makers, and analysts to explore emerging trends in decentralized computing, media and communications.

Conference sessions will be held over two days at the Palace Hotel, San Francisco, CA. We will host a special event at the Palace the evening of June 22nd.

2005 general sessions included standalone talks, "fireside chat" interveiws, panel discussions, video presentations, and other formats, featuring a stellar list of top-level industry executives and innovators. Look for information on 2006 sessions here soon.
