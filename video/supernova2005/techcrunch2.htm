Program Overview

Connected Innovators

Press Buzz

Venue












Selection Criteria: The submitted product presentation should:
  • relate to this year’s theme, Making Connections in an Increasingly Complex World;be considered a groundbreaking "connected technology" which could include a product, service or initiative that falls into either one of the distributed computing, communications, media or business categories; and,
  • have just launched, or is soon-to-be introduced to the public. The newness or "buzz-worthiness" of the idea is important to incite good word-of-mouth amongst the audience.


Marketing Benefits: Access to an unprecedented audience of influencers, including:

  • Supernova community of over 5,000 technology thought leaders.
  • The Wharton School’s extensive group of business affiliates, including alumni associations, faculty, executive students, corporate partners and its esteemed business editors from Knowledge@Wharton.
  • TechCrunch.com readership of over 20,000 technology investors, entrepreneurs, executives, developers and media.
  • Leading technology and business press.


Program Features and Pricing: Cost of participation for selected companies is $5,000. Elements of the program include:

  • Company, product description and Web site link posted on Supernova/TechCrunch Web page; company name and Web site link included in program-specific promotions to Supernova and TechCrunch audiences.
  • 5-minute product presentation during dedicated 1 hr. Connected Innovators session on Thursday, June 22nd.
  • Online coverage of product presentation by Mike Arrington, at TechCrunch.com.
  • 1 complimentary full-conference pass.
  • Technology Showcase/Connected Innovators sponsorships may be bundled for $7,000.
  • Mentioned in press materials.


Program contact: Jeanne Logozzo; techcrunch@supernova2006.com.
