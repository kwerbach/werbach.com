Last Year's Sponsors

A number of leading technology companies contributed to Supernova's success in 2004 by serving as sponsors of key conference programs. IBM Corporation was our premier sponsor and host of the Get Connected Reception and Technology Showcase. In addition to IBM, several Get Connected Sponsors displayed their cutting-edge technology innovations in the Showcase:

Cloudmark • Convoq, Inc. • dodgeball.com • LinkedIn, Ltd. • Mark Logic Corporation • Morgan, Lewis & Bockus • NextPage • Oddpost, Inc. • Santa Cruz Networks • SilkRoad technology, inc. • Six Apart, Ltd. • Socialtext
We also introduced the online Supernova Community Connection using the tools of several technology companies: Convoq, Inc., LinkedIn, Ltd., Six Apart, Ltd. and Socialtext.

And, we had help with publicizing Supernova 2004 from our media sponsors: RedHerring, MIT Technology Review and Corante, as well as our communications sponsor, Trainer Communications.
