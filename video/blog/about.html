About Werblog

This is the personal Weblog of Kevin Werbach, technology analyst, raconteur, policy wonk, and former editor of Release 1.0.

I set up my first personal Website in early 1995, around the time I created the Bare Bones Guide to HTML.  In Summer 1999, after writing about Weblogs in an article about syndication, I decided to set up one of my own.  What you're visiting is version 4.0.

Expect commentary on developments in the technology world, pointers to interesting companies I come across, and random musings about life.  I'm not sure where this site will go, but I promise to make it interesting.

You can email me at kevin@werbach.com.
